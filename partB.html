As we progress towards the future, many more inventions will probably be made, below is my own take of a possible design of something that may exist in the future that will benefit us (if it can be made). To come up with my invention, I thought of problems that people may have and made a solution accordingly using technology we may have in the future.

Introducing: Futuristic Flying Storage Box

The "Flying Box" is a revolutionary item that acts as a backpack drone that has many components that makes it reliable and convenient for day to day life. Some of the components include:

  • - Follows you with flight
  • - Uses very quiet flying method to not disturb others
  • - Can decrease weight load when moving
  • - Hold items (Main Purpose)
  • - Has scanner to instantly find needed item
  • - Uses xray to allow user to find items
  • - Voice commands
  • - Button command for the unfortunate (Mute)
  • - Maybe even guidance for the disabled
  • - AI Sensor to dodge objects
  • - Self powered with sun when walking outside
  • - Waterproof + Fireproof
  • - Autolock to present stealing.
  • - High security requirments if needed.
